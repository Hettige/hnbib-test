Dansala 2025 — HNB Investment Bank Gives Back

2025·2 min read·HNB Investment Bank
DS

In the spirit of generosity and community, HNB Investment Bank hosted its annual Dansala in 2025 — offering refreshments and meals to hundreds of visitors, passers-by, and community members in the spirit of the Vesak season.

The Dansala, a cherished Buddhist tradition of offering free meals and refreshments to the public during the Vesak festival, has long been a part of HNB Investment Bank's corporate culture. The 2025 Dansala was held at the company's premises on Dharmapala Mawatha, Colombo 03, on Vesak Poya Day.

Members of staff from across HNB Investment Bank's business units volunteered to set up and manage the Dansala, serving traditional Sri Lankan meals and refreshments to hundreds of visitors throughout the day. The event was open to members of the public, office workers, and passers-by alike.

The Dansala reflects HNB Investment Bank's broader commitment to corporate social responsibility and its deep roots in Sri Lankan culture and community. The organisation believes that financial success carries with it a responsibility to give back — and the annual Dansala is one of the most tangible expressions of that belief.

HNB Investment Bank CEO commented: 'Our Dansala is one of my favourite days in the calendar. It's a reminder of why we do what we do — to serve our community and contribute to Sri Lanka's prosperity, not just through capital markets, but through acts of kindness and generosity.'
