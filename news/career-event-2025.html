Career Event 2025 — A Day of Sport and Recognition

2025·2 min read·HNB Investment Bank
CE

HNB Investment Bank's annual Career Event 2025 brought together staff across all departments for a day of sport, team building, and recognition — celebrating the talent, dedication, and camaraderie that define the organisation's culture.

The HNB Investment Bank Annual Career Event 2025 was held at a venue in the Western Province, bringing together over 150 staff members from the Group's subsidiaries — HNB Stockbrokers, HNB Securities, and Lanka Ventures — alongside head office teams for a full day of activities.

The day's programme included team sports competitions, wellness activities, and a formal awards ceremony recognising staff across categories including Outstanding Performer, Best Team, and Service Excellence. The awards programme, now in its fifth year, has become a cornerstone of HNB Investment Bank's employee recognition culture.

Senior management used the occasion to reflect on the organisation's performance over the past year and share the strategic direction for the year ahead. Interactive sessions gave staff the opportunity to engage directly with leadership and contribute ideas to the organisation's ongoing development.

HNB Investment Bank Group CEO remarked: 'Our Career Event is one of the highlights of our year. It's a chance to step outside our day-to-day roles and remember that we are, first and foremost, a team. The talent in this room is what makes HNB Investment Bank what it is, and recognising that talent is both a privilege and a responsibility.'

The event reinforced HNB Investment Bank's commitment to building a workplace culture where performance is celebrated, well-being is prioritised, and every team member feels valued and connected to the organisation's mission.
