HNB Investment Bank Extends Collaboration with CFA Society Sri Lanka as Silver Partner

2024·3 min read·HNB Investment Bank
CFA

HNB Investment Bank has renewed its partnership with CFA Society Sri Lanka as a Silver Partner, reaffirming its commitment to advancing investment professionalism and financial education across Sri Lanka's capital markets community.

CFA Society Sri Lanka, the local member society of the CFA Institute, plays a pivotal role in upholding investment standards, promoting ethical practice, and supporting the professional development of finance practitioners across the island. HNB Investment Bank's continued partnership as Silver Partner reflects a shared commitment to these values.

The partnership includes support for CFA Society Sri Lanka's flagship events, including the Annual Investment Conference, CFA Charter Award Ceremony, and professional development workshops covering topics such as portfolio management, ESG integration, and fixed income analytics.

HNB Investment Bank CEO stated: 'Our people are our most valuable asset, and we are committed to investing in their professional development. Our partnership with CFA Society Sri Lanka is a natural extension of that commitment, and we look forward to continuing this collaboration.'

HNB Investment Bank employs a number of CFA Charterholders across its research, corporate finance, and wealth management teams. The organisation actively sponsors candidates pursuing the CFA designation and provides study support through internal learning programmes.

The CFA designation, awarded by the CFA Institute, is widely recognised as the gold standard in investment management and analysis. Sri Lanka's growing cohort of Charterholders is a positive indicator of the deepening sophistication of the country's financial services sector.
