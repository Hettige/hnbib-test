HNB Investment Bank Promotes Hayleys Rs 7 Billion Debenture Issue as Joint Placement Agent

2022·3 min read·HNB Investment Bank
HL

HNB Investment Bank acted as Joint Placement Agent for Hayleys PLC's Rs 7 billion listed debenture issue — one of the largest corporate debenture offerings in Sri Lanka's capital market history — achieving full subscription from institutional and retail investors.

Hayleys PLC, one of Sri Lanka's largest and most diversified conglomerates with interests spanning manufacturing, consumer products, and services, mandated HNB Investment Bank as one of its Joint Placement Agents for a Rs 7 billion listed debenture issue.

The debenture offering was structured in multiple tranches with varying tenors and coupon rates to appeal to the broadest possible investor base — from retail investors seeking regular income to institutional investors such as insurance companies, unit trusts, and pension funds managing duration risk.

HNB Investment Bank's distribution capabilities across institutional and retail channels were deployed to promote the issue, leveraging its relationships with licensed banks, investment managers, and high-net-worth clients. The HNB Group's extensive branch network also facilitated retail investor participation.

The offering achieved full subscription, reflecting strong investor confidence in the Hayleys Group's credit quality, business diversification, and track record of debt service. The proceeds were deployed towards refinancing existing borrowings and funding working capital requirements across the Group's business units.

This mandate added to HNB Investment Bank's growing track record of large-scale debt capital market transactions, cementing its reputation as a leading placement agent for investment-grade corporate debenture issues in Sri Lanka.
