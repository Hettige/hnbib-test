HNB Investment Bank and Serendib Frontier Capital Spearhead Full Subscription of Singer Finance LKR 2 Bn Subordinated Debt Trust Certificate Offering

2024·4 min read·HNB Investment Bank
SD

HNB Investment Bank, acting as Joint Managers and Placement Agents alongside Serendib Frontier Capital, successfully closed the LKR 2 billion subordinated debt trust certificate offering for Singer Finance (Lanka) PLC — achieving full subscription ahead of the closing date.

Singer Finance (Lanka) PLC, a leading consumer finance company listed on the Colombo Stock Exchange, sought to strengthen its Tier 2 capital base through a subordinated debt instrument. HNB Investment Bank was mandated as Joint Manager and Placement Agent alongside Serendib Frontier Capital to structure and distribute the offering.

The offering comprised LKR 2 billion of 5-year subordinated debt trust certificates at a fixed coupon, structured to qualify as Tier 2 regulatory capital under the Central Bank of Sri Lanka's capital adequacy framework. The instrument was targeted at institutional investors, high-net-worth individuals, and corporate treasury accounts.

HNB Investment Bank's distribution network and institutional investor relationships were instrumental in achieving full subscription. The offering was oversubscribed within the book-building period, with bids received from insurance companies, pension funds, licensed banks, and approved finance companies.

This transaction underscores HNB Investment Bank's standing as a leading debt capital markets arranger in Sri Lanka, having closed multiple subordinated debt, debenture, and commercial paper mandates across the banking, finance, and corporate sectors.

Singer Finance CEO commented: 'The success of this offering reflects the strong confidence our investors place in Singer Finance's balance sheet and growth trajectory. We are grateful to HNB Investment Bank and Serendib Frontier Capital for their professional execution and deep investor reach.'
