HNB Investment Bank Facilitates Sri Lanka's First-Ever High-Yield Bond Issuance for Fintrex Finance

2023·5 min read·HNB Investment Bank
FX

In a landmark first for Sri Lanka's debt capital markets, HNB Investment Bank successfully structured and distributed Sri Lanka's first-ever high-yield bond — a LKR-denominated instrument for Fintrex Finance Limited, opening a new asset class for domestic institutional investors.

Sri Lanka's debt capital markets reached a significant milestone with the issuance of the country's first domestically structured high-yield bond. Fintrex Finance Limited, a rapidly growing non-bank financial institution, mandated HNB Investment Bank as Sole Arranger and Placement Agent for the landmark transaction.

The instrument was structured as a 3-year fixed-rate bond carrying an above-market coupon, reflecting the higher credit risk profile consistent with international high-yield market conventions. The offering was targeted at sophisticated institutional investors capable of assessing and managing credit risk, including licensed banks, insurance companies, and fund managers.

HNB Investment Bank's credit research team prepared a comprehensive credit assessment of Fintrex Finance, benchmarking the issuer's financial ratios, asset quality, and growth trajectory against regional comparables. The offering memorandum was prepared to international standards, enabling institutional investors to conduct rigorous due diligence.

The successful placement demonstrated the latent investor appetite for higher-yielding credit instruments in Sri Lanka's fixed income market — a segment that had historically been underserved by the dominance of government securities and investment-grade corporate debentures.

This transaction is expected to pave the way for further high-yield issuances by mid-market corporates seeking growth capital beyond the traditional banking channel, deepening Sri Lanka's corporate bond market and providing investors with a new risk-return profile.
