HNB Investment Bank Powers Alpha Fire Services' Journey from Historic CSE Listing to Sale of Controlling Interest

2023·4 min read·HNB Investment Bank
AF

HNB Investment Bank served as the exclusive financial advisor to Alpha Fire Services (Pvt) Ltd across two landmark transactions — its initial public offering on the Colombo Stock Exchange and the subsequent strategic sale of a controlling interest to a foreign investor — marking a full investment banking lifecycle engagement.

Alpha Fire Services, one of Sri Lanka's leading fire safety and suppression companies, embarked on a transformative corporate journey with HNB Investment Bank as its strategic financial advisor. The engagement began with a mandate to list the company on the Colombo Stock Exchange through an Initial Public Offering.

HNB Investment Bank's corporate finance team worked closely with Alpha Fire Services' management to prepare the company for its public market debut — restructuring its balance sheet, preparing the prospectus, coordinating regulatory approvals with the SEC and CSE, and managing the bookbuilding and investor roadshow processes. The IPO was successfully completed, raising significant growth capital and creating a liquid market for the company's shares.

Following the listing, HNB Investment Bank was retained to advise on a strategic transaction — the sale of a controlling interest in Alpha Fire Services to a regional strategic investor. This M&A mandate required HNB Investment Bank's team to run a competitive auction process, prepare an information memorandum, engage with international bidders, and negotiate transaction terms.

The dual mandate demonstrates HNB Investment Bank's full-service investment banking capability — from equity capital markets and regulatory advisory through to sell-side M&A execution. The transaction created significant value for Alpha Fire Services' shareholders while securing a strategic partner to drive the company's regional expansion.

This deal reinforces HNB Investment Bank's position as the go-to advisor for mid-market Sri Lankan companies seeking to access capital markets and pursue strategic transactions.
