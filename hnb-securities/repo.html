How a Repo Works

1

You Invest

Transfer funds to HNB Securities. Minimum LKR 250,000, same-day processing.

2

We Sell Securities

We sell you Government securities (T-Bills/T-Bonds) as collateral for your investment.

3

Interest Accrues

Your agreed repo rate accrues daily over the selected tenor — overnight to 90 days.

4

Repurchase + Return

At maturity, we repurchase the securities and return your principal plus net interest.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment for a repo?

The minimum investment for an overnight or 7-day repo is LKR 250,000. For 14-day and 30-day repos, the minimum is LKR 500,000. For 90-day repos, the minimum is LKR 1,000,000.

How is interest taxed?

Withholding tax (WHT) at 10% is deducted at source on the gross interest earned. You will receive a WHT certificate for your tax records. No further income tax liability arises if you are an individual resident investor.

Can I redeem before maturity?

Term repos (7-day and above) are generally held to maturity. Early redemption may be accommodated subject to a break-funding adjustment. Overnight repos mature and are credited the next business day.

What securities are used as collateral?

HNB Securities uses Treasury Bills and Treasury Bonds issued by the Government of Sri Lanka as collateral. These are sovereign instruments with zero credit risk, auctioned by the Central Bank of Sri Lanka.

How do I invest — what accounts do I need?

You need a HNB Securities client account (KYC/AML compliant) and a linked bank account for fund transfers. Contact our team at +94 112 206 220 or email securities@hnbib.lk to open an account.
