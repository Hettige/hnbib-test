Why T-Bills?

Treasury Bills are short-term Government of Sri Lanka securities issued at a discount to face value. At maturity, you receive the full face value — the difference being your return.

  • ✓
    Zero Credit Risk — sovereign guarantee of the GoSL
  • ✓
    Short Durations — 91, 182, or 364 days
  • ✓
    Competitive Yields — above typical FD rates
  • ✓
    CDS Holding — dematerialised, held in your CDS account
  • ✓
    Min LKR 100,000 — accessible to retail investors
