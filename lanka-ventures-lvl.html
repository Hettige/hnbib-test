Two CSE-listed funds managed by HNB Investment Bank — Lanka Ventures, Sri Lanka's pioneer venture capital vehicle, and LVL Energy Fund, focused on the energy sector.

Our Story

Three decades of venture capital in Sri Lanka.

Lanka Ventures PLC was incorporated in 1992 as Sri Lanka's first institutionally-backed venture capital fund, listed on the Colombo Stock Exchange in 1995.

In 2006, the fund pivoted to the energy sector, giving rise to LVL Energy Fund PLC — a separately listed vehicle that channels capital into renewable and conventional energy projects across Sri Lanka.

Both funds are managed and administered by HNB Investment Bank under its fund management business line.

Our Story

Pioneer. Pivot. Platform.

Lanka Ventures PLC was Sri Lanka's first institutionally-backed venture capital fund, established in 1992 and listed on the CSE in 1995. After more than a decade of VC investments, the fund made a strategic pivot to the energy sector in 2006 — launching LVL Energy Fund PLC as a separately listed vehicle focused on renewable and conventional energy assets.

Both funds are managed by HNB Investment Bank, bringing institutional rigour and capital markets depth to Sri Lanka's alternative investment landscape.
